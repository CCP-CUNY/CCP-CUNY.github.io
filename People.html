Timothy Ricker

Accepting Ph.D. Applications for Fall 2019

Lab Website | Campus Website | contact: Timothy.Ricker@csi.cuny.edu

Assistant Professor | College of Staten Island

Working Memory, Multi-Tasking, Attention, Consolidation

The Memory & Attention Lab at CUNY investigates how information within the working memory system is maintained and forgotten. We use behavioral experimentation, mathematical process modeling, and Bayesian estimation in support of this goal. More specific areas of investigation include the effects of time, multi-tasking, and complexity on short-term retention.
